Tips

How to avoid speeding and accidents

  • Take your time
  • Be mindful of the speed limit and your speedometer
  • Do not be influenced by other drivers
  • Avoid peer pressure
  • Buy a safe/modern car
  • Turn your mobile off
  • Avoid distractions
  • Use cruise control
